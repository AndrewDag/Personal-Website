COLLABORATIVE DESIGN

AI-Based Chat Service

8/23 - 12/23

Summary

As apart of the Michigan State University Computer Science Capstone experience, I am working with RPM, a supply chain and logistics company, to develop an AI Chatbot that would help improve their customer efficiency and reduce the need for a 24/7 on-call representitive. The Chatbot is a backend service that will give dynamic responses to users and offer a variety of different questions that it can answer. The Chatbot will be able to answer FAQ-style questions, but it also has the ability to pull information off of RPM's Turvo database. This database is a .NET C# database that holds information about RPM's shipments, orders, and more. The Chatbot will be able to recieve the information from the database through API calls to the Turvo database and report it back to the user.
